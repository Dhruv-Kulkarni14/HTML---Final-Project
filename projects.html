Our Projects

Covid Safety Kit

The Covid Safety Kit project is aimed at providing essential safety supplies to individuals and families in underprivileged communities. Each kit contains masks, hand sanitizers, disinfectant wipes, gloves, and informational pamphlets on how to stay safe during the pandemic. By distributing these kits, we aim to reduce the spread of Covid-19 and protect vulnerable populations who may not have easy access to these critical supplies.

Library Books

The Library Books project focuses on improving literacy and education in underprivileged communities by providing access to a wide range of books. We collect new and gently used books from donors and distribute them to schools, community centers, and libraries in need. The project emphasizes the importance of reading and aims to foster a love for books among children and adults alike, enhancing their educational opportunities and personal growth.

Free Tutoring

The Free Tutoring project offers academic support to students who are struggling with their studies. Our team of dedicated volunteers provides one-on-one and group tutoring sessions in subjects like math, science, reading, and writing. These sessions are designed to help students understand difficult concepts, improve their grades, and build confidence in their academic abilities. The project targets students from low-income families who may not have access to private tutoring services.
